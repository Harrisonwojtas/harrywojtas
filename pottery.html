2025 Winter/Spring Works

A long and warm winter. I've been working with a darker clay body, Oregon Red Smooth. It is a cone 5 clay. Very Smoooooooooth. Focused on cups, mugs, jugs, pots.

Have been working on cone 10 pieces. Waiting until end of studio time for those to be fired in the kiln. Playing with jug shapes, pots, and exploring the glazes and glaze combos in the cone 10 repitore. Sticking with reduction fires. Also worked on more scultupure building which has been fun and outside of my confort zone. Stay tuned for those photos once they go through the kiln!
