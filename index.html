Welcome to my art website. My name is Harry. I am a ceramicist, artist, scientist, based in Sitka Alaska. When Im not working in the lab, I try to spend my time carving blocks, throwing pots, or taking photos. This is a place I can display my current and past works for y'all.

Currently working in the ceramic studio working on lamps, baking pots, and jugs.

I believe in keeping things simple and letting the work speak for itself. Thanks for stopping by!
